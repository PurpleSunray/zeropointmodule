Arist Biography

Zero Point Module is the psy trance project of Manuel Frener & Hannes Goster (The Antiker) and Matthias Frener (Purple Sunray).
After playing psytrance DJ-sets for many years, we decided start our own project in 2014. Our mission is to flood the floor with hard-hitting, twisted, high-enegry sound.
Our debut E.P. "Initial Singularity" was released on Sarnarshourt Rec. in Feb. 2019.
Stay tuned for more... :)

Zero Point Module - The Story

Millions of years ago, on a far away galaxy, an ancient alien race discovered how to use a tremendous source of energy - the Zero Point Energy. They decided to not provide this technology to the whole univerese, because not all races might be ready to handle it.
Instead they sent spaceships, that were equipped with Zero Point Modules, out into the universe. They had the aim to find civilizations and planets that were ready to use it without destroying themself.
A ZPM mothership has arrived on planet earth and started experiments on the human race, by modulating acoustic sound frequencies and mindbendning effects. The first results were very positive, so they fully recharged a ZPM to - Intial Singularity - and continued with the local research project at ZPM laboratories (aka ZPM Labs).
Njoy! :)
